Haptic Feedback Project - XAMK Game Studios (2023)

3D Artist & Sound Designer

In 2023, I worked at XAMK Game Studios as a 3D Artist and Sound Designer on the Haptic Feedback Project, a mech sci-fi VR experience exploring the use of haptic technology in gameplay.
The project focuses on creating a tactile experience of piloting a giant mech on a desert planet. Through haptic feedback, players can feel the vibrations of the mech walking, collecting materials, firing weapons, and taking damage from environmental hazards and enemies.
The game also explores the limits of haptic technology and investigates how tactile feedback influences immersion and VR-related simulation sickness.

Team:
